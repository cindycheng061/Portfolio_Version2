About me

Woman pursing - software development

In the past six months, my journey with She Codes has been an exhilarating learning adventure. I've delved into HTML/CSS, Python, Django/DRF, JS and React, immersing myself in a fast-paced and challenging process.

Originally from China, I began my career as an electrical maintenance engineer at a nuclear power station, honing my problem-solving skills. After moving to Australia, I transitioned into the telecommunications industry as a field service technician, where I continued to tackle technical challenges. The satisfaction of hands-on work and the pride in finding solutions have always fueled my passion.

Being part of the SheCodes community has been a rewarding experience. Despite the program's intensity, I've gained confidence, skills, new friends, and invaluable opportunities. I'm well aware that the road ahead will be challenging, but I'm committed to continuous learning and unwavering coding practice. SheCodes has become an integral part of my life, offering a unique opportunity to excel in the tech world. This experience has fueled tremendous growth, and I eagerly anticipate channeling this passion and skill into my future career.
